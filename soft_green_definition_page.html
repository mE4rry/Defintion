•san to spíti mouGreek

[like my home]

This phrase encapsulates the feeling that someone is your home, your place of comfort and belonging. It conveys the sense of safety, warmth, and love one feels in the presence of a particular person, akin to the feelings associated with being at home. It's a deeply emotional expression, reflecting the idea that home is not just a physical place but can also be a person who provides a sense of security and peace.

Example: Whenever she sees her brother, she feels 'san to spíti mou'.
